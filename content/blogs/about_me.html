I was born in Romania and spent my childhood and teenage years in Bucharest. In my free time, I enjoy travelling, going to the gym, walking around the city and discovering new places and going out with my friends

Education

After high school, I started my Bachelors in Systems Engineering at Polytechnica University of Bucharest. I graduated from college in June 2022, right before starting my Masters at London Business School.

Work experience

I started my professional career as a Software Engineer working at a VC-backed start-up. After 10 months in the role, I realized programming is not what I want to do going forward so I started to explore other career paths and look for a more social role. I decided to go into Venture Capital and joined Speedinvest, an early-stage VC fund with over 500m AUM.
